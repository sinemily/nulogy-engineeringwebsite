Self-Managed Teams

Our product development teams include developers, a product designer, a product manager, and an agile tester.

With our self-managed structure, you and your team are in control of how you work together. When a new feature comes in, you decide how to best build it.

Build projects around motivated individuals. Give them the environment and support they need, and trust them to get the job done.

- Agile Principle

Team Safety

Divergent opinions are inevitable. But we don’t fear conflict, we embrace it.

We believe you should have the safety to fearlessly speak your mind, disagree, fail, admit you don’t know. That’s important to us.

Nulogites regularly solicit feedback from people within and outside of their team. We encourage everyone, from executives to individuals, to ask for help. It’s okay to not know everything. Team members regularly question assumptions to make sure we’ve thought through all solutions. People may disagree, but we empathetically work together to figure out the best path forward.

Read more about safety in an employee's blog post.

Technical Safety

Alongside team safety comes technical safety. You might break things. It happens. What’s more important is how we deal with it.

We take measures to ensure that developers work in a fail-friendly environment.

For instance, we developed an incident management system that works to find the root cause of major incidents. We use this to learn and improve, not to blame.
